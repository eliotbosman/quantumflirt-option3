About

We are Quantum Flirt productions.
As multi-instrumentalitsts, audio engineers and visual artists with 20 years experience in the field, we value the deep relationship between image and sound.
We specialise in composing and producing original music, custom made and tailored to your needs. We have experience crafting sonic storytelling for short films, interactive media, games, commercials and performing arts.

Clients include: Studio Moniker for the Stedelijk Museum, Mozilla, M+ Hong Kong, NPO, Het Nieuw Instituut, Feeld, Stimuleringsfonds, Qiu Yang for:
Tiffany & Co.
